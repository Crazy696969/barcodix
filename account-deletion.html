Account Deletion

Barcodix

Barcodix allows users to permanently delete their account and associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Barcodix app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your authentication information will be removed.
  • Your personal profile information will be deleted.
  • Your saved application data will be removed from our database.

Data Retention

Some technical records may be retained for a limited period if required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes. These records do not contain information that allows normal use of the application.

Need Help?

If you experience any issues while deleting your account, please contact us through our Support page.
